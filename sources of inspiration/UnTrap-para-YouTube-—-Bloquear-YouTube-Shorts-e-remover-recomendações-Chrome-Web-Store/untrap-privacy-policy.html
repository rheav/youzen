Privacy Notice

UnTrap works locally by default.
You can use UnTrap without an account in Local Mode. Optional cloud features may transmit your email address, extension settings, or the URL of the current YouTube video only if you choose to enable them.

Local Mode (default)

When using UnTrap without an account, interface customization features and settings work locally in your browser.
No personal data is transmitted to our servers in Local Mode.

Optional Cloud Features

If you enable any of these:

  • Account / Sync - May transmit your email address, authentication data, and extension settings to enable account access and cross-device sync.
  • Video Summarization - May transmit the URL of the current YouTube video when you request a summary. Generated summaries may be stored anonymously and are not linked to your identity.

All transfers are encrypted using HTTPS and occur only if you choose to enable optional cloud features.
