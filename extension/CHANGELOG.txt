youZen - Changelog

================================================================================

youZen is a Chrome extension for people who want YouTube to stop wasting
their time. One sidepanel, a pile of toggles, and a blocklist — kill Shorts,
strip comments, silence related videos, hide the like/dislike numbers, and
block any channel or keyword that ever makes you groan.

No tracking. No accounts. No sync. Your settings live in local storage and
never leave the browser.

🎯 CORE FEATURES:

  🚫 Hide Shorts Everywhere
     Kill Shorts on the homepage, left sidebar, channel tabs, search results,
     and the Subscriptions feed. One master toggle, or cherry-pick per surface.

  🧹 Declutter the Homepage
     Hide the main feed (empty / quick-links card / redirect to Subscriptions),
     the category chip bar, and breaking-news shelves.

  🔍 Declutter Search
     Drop the "People also watched" and mixed shelves so search stays a list
     of videos, not an ad for sidequests.

  🎬 Watch Page, Calmer
     Hide related videos, end-screen cards, the autoplay toggle, merch and
     ticket shelves, the "Join" / member button, the per-channel notification
     bell, the Share/Save/Ask action bar, the like/dislike segmented button,
     and collapse the description by default — all individual toggles.

  💬 Comments, Your Call
     Hide comments, live chat, or both. Nothing renders until you ask for it.

  🧭 Navigation Chrome
     Hide the full left sidebar, the masthead notification bell, the trending
     and explore entries, and the "Create" button in the topbar.

  ⛔ Blocklist by Keyword or Channel
     Right-click any selected text on YouTube to block it as a substring rule
     or a channel rule. Matched cards vanish; the feed reflows.

  ↪️ Redirect Shorts to /watch
     Shorts open as normal video pages — full playback controls, no vertical
     scroll cage.

  ⏸️ Per-Tab Pause & Global Kill-Switch
     Right-click the page to pause youZen on just that tab. Use the global
     toggle to turn everything off at once, without losing your settings.

  🎨 Light & Dark Themes
     Auto-follows your system theme, or pick one. Warm red-to-yellow accent
     either way.

  🔒 Zero Data Collection
     No telemetry, no accounts, no external requests. Permissions are the
     minimum needed: sidepanel, storage, context menus, and YouTube host
     access. That's it.

🆕 19/04/2026 - v1.0.0j Released:

INITIAL RELEASE.

NEW - CSS-Attribute Engine:
- Flicker-free: rules fire at document_start before YouTube paints
- Every toggle maps to a data-ytc-* attribute on <html>
- 25 individually togglable features across Feeds + Watch tabs

NEW - Feature Panel (Sidepanel):
- Grouped toggles with master/sub indeterminate states
- Home-feed mode picker: Empty / Quick-links card / Redirect to Subs
- Light / Dark / Auto theme with warm accent gradient

NEW - Blocklist:
- Keyword rules (substring, default case-insensitive) + channel rules
- Right-click "Block selection" context menu entries
- Live MutationObserver sweeps new cards as they load
- Scoped to card-level selectors so layout never breaks

NEW - Control:
- Per-tab pause (right-click → pause on this tab)
- Global kill-switch that un-applies every attribute instantly
- Reset-to-defaults from the Options menu

NEW - Stability:
- 217 unit + integration tests, including 29 CSS-rule assertions that
  prove every selector in src/styles/youtube.css hides what it claims
- Non-destructive storage top-up on extension updates: your settings
  survive, new keys get sensible defaults
- 71 KB gzipped bundle

KNOWN LIMITATIONS:
- Mobile DOM (m.youtube.com) is out of scope: youZen only matches
  www.youtube.com so the mobile site stays untouched
- A handful of selectors target language-invariant attributes (icon SVG
  paths, aria titles); if YouTube renames a container tomorrow, expect
  a patch release within a day or two
